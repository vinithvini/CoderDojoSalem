About us

CoderDojo has spread across the World with massive result .It makes children to learn coding in a easy way. Many children are benefited through this CoderDojo. Now there are nearly 1300 centers across world. In India there are 14 centers, And we are the Third in Tamil Nadu.

We teach how to code, develop websites, apps, programs, games and explore technology in a formal and creative environment. In addition to learning code, attendees meet like minded people and are exposed to the possibilities of technology.
