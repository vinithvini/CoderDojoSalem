CoderDojoSalem

CoderDojoSalem is a worldwide movement of free , volunteer-led, community-based programming clubs for young people in Salem, India. Anyone aged seven to seventeen can visit a Dojo where they can learn to code, build a website, create an app or a game, and explore technology in an informal, creative, and social environment.
